### Y68/D20 smartwatch 
Y68/D20 Smartwatch Is a Smartwatch That where people use it as smartwatch. The features (apps) typically pre-installed are:
Watch Face: The main screen showing the time and date.
Step Counter (Pedometer): Tracks your daily steps.
Sports Mode: Usually includes simple modes like Running, Sit-ups, or Jumping Rope.
Heart Rate Monitor: Manually triggered to measure your pulse.
Blood Pressure/Oxygen (SpO2): Note: These are often "simulated" sensors on these budget models and are not medically accurate.
Message/Notification View: This isn't an "app" you open to type, but a screen that displays alerts forwarded from your phone (SMS, WhatsApp, etc.).
System/Settings: A menu often used to find information about the watch (MAC address, firmware version) or to power it off.
